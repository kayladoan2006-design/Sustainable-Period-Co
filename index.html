About Us

Sustainable Period Co. was created to provide period products that are affordable, comfortable, and better for the environment. We believe small changes can make a big difference when it comes to reducing waste.

Our History

Sustainable Period Co. was founded in 2020 by Maya Johnson, a college student who noticed how expensive period products were and how much plastic waste came from single-use menstrual items. The company first started by selling reusable pads online to friends, classmates, and local community members.

Over time, the business expanded to include menstrual cups, period underwear, organic cotton products, and educational kits. Today, Sustainable Period Co. serves schools, community organizations, and individual customers across the United States.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make menstrual care more sustainable, affordable, and less embarrassing to talk about. We want customers to feel confident choosing products that are better for their bodies, their budgets, and the environment.

Connection to the Sustainable Development Goals

  • Good Health and Well-Being: We provide safer and reusable menstrual products.
  • Gender Equality: We support menstrual equity and access to period care.
  • Responsible Consumption: We reduce waste from disposable products.
  • Climate Action: We encourage lower-waste habits in everyday life.

Why Choose Us?

Customers choose Sustainable Period Co. because we combine comfort, education, and sustainability. Our products are designed to last longer than disposable options, which helps customers save money over time. We also donate a portion of our profits to programs that provide free menstrual products to people in need.

  • Affordable reusable menstrual products
  • Simple, clean, and comfortable designs
  • Eco-friendly packaging
  • Educational resources for schools and communities
  • Support for menstrual equity programs
