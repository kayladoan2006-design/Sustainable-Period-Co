Company Policies

Financing Policy

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.

Delivery Policy

Orders are processed within 1–3 business days. Standard shipping usually arrives within 5–7 business days. Customers will receive tracking information once their order has been shipped. Free shipping is available on orders over $50.

Return Policy

Unused and unopened products may be returned within 30 days of purchase for a full refund. Due to health and safety regulations, opened menstrual products cannot be returned. If a product arrives damaged or defective, customers should contact us within 7 days for a replacement or refund.

Privacy Policy

We respect customer privacy and never sell personal information to third parties. Customer information is used only to process orders, provide support, and improve our services.
